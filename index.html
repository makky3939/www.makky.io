Hello,

I am a 1st-year doctoral student at the Graduate School of Library, Information and Media Studies, University of Tsukuba. My current research interest is Human-Machine collaboration in Crowdsourcing for efficient microtask processing.

I am also interested in developing web applications and, I am fluent in JavaScript, TypeScript, Ruby, and Python. I have experienced as a part-time engineer and an intern in more than ten companies.
